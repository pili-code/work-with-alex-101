# GTM Sync analysis — what Alex asks

Source: GTM Sync transcript, Jun 4 2026 (Fireflies id `01KSNY3Z26XV90XE6NQXPV82QT`).
Participants: Alessandro "Alex" Alter, Dianne Alter, Pilar "Pili" Ratti. 491 lines.

## Alex's behavior, by bucket

### 1. Conclusion first, skip the process (his #1 behavior)
> "What was the conclusion? All the process to get to the conclusion is not particularly relevant.
> We can ask later. Just give us the conclusion so that we can streamline the conversation and talk
> about what matters."

He interrupts narrative and asks for the decision/result directly.

### 2. Numbers — specifically conversion rates, and whether they're tracked
- "What's the conversion rate of the community landing page right now? Total visits to total subscribers."
- "Were you planning on getting traffic to TDP Labs through YouTube? And Google Analytics is installed, right?"
- He won't trust a number unless the tracking is verified ("you pull up the terminal and see if it calls Google").

### 3. Segmentation — "who exactly"
- "All the Beehive subscribers we have right now are not customers, right?"
- "Can you create a separate list only for customers?"
- "Sorry, who was the one we don't feel good about? Why?" — digs into the specific deal and the reason.

### 4. Channel coverage + bias to action
- "You tried LinkedIn too?"
- "Just do it. We can." / "Let's get going with that one, otherwise we get stuck."

## What a business/numbers boss will most likely ask (pre-empt)
- Conversion rate at each funnel step (visits → signups → customers).
- How many are paying customers vs. just subscribers.
- Is it tracked properly (GA/UTM firing)?
- $ value of the pipeline change this week.
- Which channel did each lead come from.
- Why a deal is stalled and what the next action is.
- "What do you need from me to unblock this?"

## Agenda improvements (mapped to his requests)
1. Bottom Line box at the top: what changed, the one number, the one decision needed.
2. A number/CR next to every line item.
3. Pipeline table with a "Why / status reason" column.
4. Tag every item Decision / FYI / Blocked.
5. "Tracking confirmed? Y/N" next to any new channel/page.
6. Cut process notes; lead with the outcome, move backstory to an appendix.
